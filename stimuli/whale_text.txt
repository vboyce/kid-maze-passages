Humpback whales are known for their complex songs and exciting jumps out of the water. 
[humpback_sing.jpg |]
They are also some of the most creative hunters in the ocean. 
These whales use bubbles to trap and catch fish, in a hunting technique called bubble net feeding.
[humpback_bubble_4.jpg |]
Here's how it works. 
A whale dives below a school of fish. 
It swims in a slow upward spiral, blowing air through its blowhole. 
The bubbles rise to the surface and form a curtain. 
[humpback_bubble_3.jpg |]
It is like a net made of air, and the fish trapped inside can't escape. 
Then the whale lunges up through the middle with its mouth open wide. 
[humpback_bubble_2.jpg |]
It swallows an enormous mouthful of fish in one gulp.
Some humpback whales do this alone, but others hunt in teams. 
[humpback_bubble_1.jpg |]
When they work together, they take on different roles. 
Some whales produce the bubbles, and others herd the fish toward the center.
[humpback_bubble_6.jpg|]
Some even make loud calls that may help herd the fish tighter. 
Each whale has a role, and the whole team has to coordinate for the hunt to succeed.
[humpback_bubble_7.jpg|]
Scientists tracked hundreds of individual humpback whales in British Columbia, Canada, over twenty years.
Humpback whales have distinctive tail markings which scientists used to tell individual whales apart. 
[humpback_tail.jpg|]
The scientists tracked which whales spent time together.
They also noted which whales knew how to bubble net feed and when they fed alone or together.
[three_humpbacks.jpg|]
They found strong evidence that the bubble net technique spreads through social contact. 
Whales that spent more time together were far more likely to both use it. 
[humpback_bubble_5.jpg |]
This suggests that bubble net feeding is not something whales are born knowing or that they invent on their own. 
[humpback_calf.jpg|]
It is a skill that is learned by watching and copying other whales. 
This is similar to how you might learn how to cook by watching family members in the kitchen. 
[humpback_bubble_8.jpg|]
